### Display Mode

[Built-in Filters](https://github.com/OpenEmu/OpenEmu/wiki/User-guide:-Filters): 
* 2xBR
* 4xBR
* Beam4
* Blinky
* CRT
* LCD3x
* Linear
* Nearest Neighbor
* NTSC
* NTSC (Blargg)
* Pixellate (This is the default filter)
* SABR
* Scanline
